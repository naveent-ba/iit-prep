## What is an "Invariant"? (The Key Idea)

Before we go further, let's understand this word — it's the foundation of everything.

**Invariant = Something that does NOT change, even when everything else changes.**

### Everyday Examples

| Situation | What Changes | What Stays the Same (Invariant) |
|:----------|:-------------|:--------------------------------|
| Pour water from glass to bottle | Shape of water | **Amount of water** |
| Stretch a rubber band | Length, width | **Material (rubber)** |
| Rotate a triangle | Position, orientation | **Sum of angles = 180°** |
| Exchange ₹500 for coins | Form of money | **Total value = ₹500** |

### Why This Matters for JEE

Every JEE problem creates a situation where many things change. **The trick is to find what doesn't change.**

- In **Physics**: Energy is conserved. Momentum is conserved. Charge is conserved.
- In **Math**: Certain relationships stay true no matter how you transform a shape or equation.
- In **Chemistry**: Atoms are conserved. Electrons flow to create stable bonds.

> **The student who finds the invariant solves in 2 lines.**
> **The student who tracks every change solves in 2 pages.**

## The Solution: Invariance Thinking

These guides are built on one question:

> **"What changes? What remains the same?"**

Every subject has quantities that nature or logic **refuses to change**. If you learn to spot these, you can solve problems you've never seen before.

| Subject | What Stays Constant |
|:--------|:--------------------|
| **Mathematics** | Structure under transformation (relationships between parts) |
| **Physics** | Conservation laws (Energy, Momentum, Charge) |
| **Chemistry** | Stability rules and Coulomb's Law ($q/r^2$) |

## The Core Philosophy

> **"Don't memorize the solution. Recognize what stays constant."**

When you see a problem:
1. Ask: What is conserved / unchanged here?
2. Ask: What transformation is happening?
3. The answer follows from the constraint.

This is how top rankers think. This is what separates AIR 100 from AIR 10,000.
